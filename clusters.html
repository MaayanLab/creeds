D3 Clustergram

About:
Zoom into the clustergram using scroll. Reorder the clustergram using the toggle buttons or by clicking rows or columns. Double-click to programatically reset zoom/panning. Search for a gene to zoom into the matrix. Change the size of the row/colum groups by moving the sliders. Click on row/column groups. See README for more information.
D3 Clustergram is developed by Nick Fernandez at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai in the Ma'ayan Lab a DCIC BD2K-LINCS center.
