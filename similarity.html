Search signatures by name

Signatures can be searched by the gene symbols of genes being perturbed, name of diseases for disease signatures, and name of drugs for drug perturbation signatures.

Examples: TP53, Breast cancer, Imatinib.

Signature search by input gene lists

Signatures that are similar to a query signature from the user can be searched. The similarity between signatures are measured by signed Jaccard Index.

Once the search results are displayed, user can select signatures of interest by clicking on them and visualize them in a clustergram.

Here you can search and prioritize signatures from the collection by either entering your own input lists of up and down genes, or by entering a term that will search the signatures meta-data
